# EC-021: 802.11 Wi‑Fi deauth/association reason codes

## Summary

Devices disconnect due to Wi‑Fi deauthentication/association issues
identified by reason codes.

## Reproduction

1. Collect `net.log` and `eventlog.txt`.
2. Identify 802.11 reason/status codes.
3. Ask what the codes mean.

## Conversation

User: “Wi‑Fi drops frequently. What do reason code 15/34 mean?”

## Expected result

- Diagnosis explains common reason/status codes.
- Evidence references codes from logs.
- Next steps suggest signal or AP tuning based on codes.

## Cleanup

- None.
