# EC-013: Chromebook battery or power issues

## Summary
Chromebooks fail to power on or charge due to battery/power state issues.

## Reproduction
1. Simulate a device that will not power on.
2. Collect device hardware status via Chrome Management API.
3. Ask for troubleshooting guidance.

## Conversation
User: “Our Chromebook won’t power on or charge.”

## Expected result
- Diagnosis references battery/power reset procedure.
- Evidence includes device hardware status fields.
- Next steps advise power cycle or support escalation.

## Cleanup
- None.
